This tool estimates your carbon footprint using the IPCC 2006 Guidelines and localized emission factors for Indonesia.

Core Formula

$$ \text{CO}_2 = \text{Activity Data} \times \text{Emission Factor} $$

Scope Breakdown

  • Scope 1: Direct emissions (e.g., vehicle fuel)
  • Scope 2: Indirect electricity (e.g., PLN grid: ~0.71 kg CO‚ÇÇ/kWh)
  • Scope 3: Optional (commuting, waste, etc.)

Example Calculation

Electricity (100 kWh):
$ 100 \text{kWh} \times 0.71 \text{kg CO}_2/\text{kWh} = 71 \text{kg CO}_2 $
